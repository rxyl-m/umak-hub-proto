Why UMAK HUB

Everything you need in one place

Built for Senior High School ICT students completing 80 hours of on-site work immersion.

Real-Time Hour Tracking
Log your daily work hours with dates and task descriptions. Watch your progress bar fill up in real time as you complete your immersion.
Journal Documentation
Write journal entries and upload files, photos, and PDFs for each day. Keep a complete record of your immersion experience.
Section Leaderboard
See how your section is doing collectively. Browse classmates' profiles and progress in a shared member directory.
Personal Portfolio
Your profile page is your immersion portfolio. Shareable, professional, and automatically updated as you log hours.
PIN-Secured Access
Each profile is protected with a personal PIN so only you can log your own hours. Secure, simple, no passwords to remember.
Works on Any Device
Fully responsive across phones, tablets, and desktops. Log hours from the office, at home, or anywhere in between.
How It Works

Up and running in minutes

Four simple steps to start tracking your immersion journey.

1
Create an Account
Sign up with your school email address and a secure password.
2
Build Your Profile
Add your name, section, and upload a profile photo.
3
Log Daily Hours
Each day, log your hours and write a journal entry about your tasks.
4
Complete Immersion
Hit 80 hours and your portfolio is ready to share with teachers and employers.
